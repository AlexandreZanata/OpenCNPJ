## Choosing a license

| Concern | MIT | Apache 2.0 |
|---------|-----|------------|
| Simplicity | Minimal text, widely understood | Longer, enterprise-standard |
| Patent grant | Implicit (jurisdiction-dependent) | Explicit patent grant + termination clause |
| Attribution | Copyright notice required | Copyright + NOTICE file for derivatives |
| Corporate adoption | Common in libraries | Preferred by many enterprises |

**Recommendation:** Use MIT for small integrations; use Apache 2.0 when your legal team requires an explicit patent grant.
